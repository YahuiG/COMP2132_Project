Comp 2132 - Dice Game

How to Play?

  • To start, click the 'Roll Dice' button.
  • To advance to the next round, click the'Roll Dice' button again.
  • After 3 rounds, the game is over and final score is caculated.
  • If you want to play again, click the'New Game'buyyon or refresh the page.

Rules:

  • Each round, you and the computer both roll a pair of dice.
  • Your score for that round will be base on the sum of the dice.
  • If you roll doubles(e.g. a 3 and a 3) then youe xcore is multiplied by 2.
  • Rolling a 1 will give you a score of 0 for that round.
  • The player who has the most points at the end of 3 rounds is the winner.
